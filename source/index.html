What is this site?

Colors for devs is a quick and easy way to create color palettes for your web projects. It's a tool designed for people who are not familiar with color theory and just need a ready to use color palette, so don't expect any fancy and multicolored palettes.

What about the palette?

The generated palette consists of four colors: the base color (random or user selected), an accent color (complementary) and two harmonious shades of gray (dark and light).

Below you can find some ideas of how you could use each color:

Base: self-explanatory

Accent: buttons, links and anything you want to stand out

Dark gray: texts (light background), shadows, outlines

Light gray: texts (dark background), background
